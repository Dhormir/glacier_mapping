Directions

  • Layers: To toggle between landsat channels (5-4-2), high-resolution ESRI imagery, and overall model predictions, use the tile icon on the top right of the map display.
  • Predictions: Press the Shift key to create a box within which to make predictions. When you click, the model will run, and the results will appear as a geojson layer. Press the ESC key to remove the box and exit prediction mode. If the box is not appearing, try dragging the map first.
  • Editing: To edit predicted polygons, click the Edit layers button (vertex next to a mouse icon). Click and drag a purple square to change its position. Click and drag a green square to indroduce a new vertex at that position. Click both mouse buttons on a vertex to remove it. To stop editing, click the edit button again.
  • Adding / deleting polygons: To create a new polygon, trace out its shape after pressing the New Polygon button (triangle at the top left). To delete a layer, select the eraser icon on the left and click the layer to remove.
  • Pan and zoom: Clicking and dragging pans, double clicking zooms, and thead +/- symbol zooms in and out, like in google maps.
